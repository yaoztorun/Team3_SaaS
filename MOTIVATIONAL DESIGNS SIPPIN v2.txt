-- GAMIFICATION & SELF-DETERMINATION (SDT) ---
Users need for Competence
Serve Achiever HEXAD user type
- Badges: Competence and achievement
- Streaks: Loss aversion, commitment/consistency, scarcity
- Stats: Social comparison, Achiever type
- Persuasive system design (PSD): Rewards and Recognition
- Foggs Behavioral Model (FBM): Anticipation future accomplishment

-- SOCIAL INFLUENCE & VALIDATION --
Users look at behavior other to guide own behavior
	-> usage and social engagement
- Like & Comment & Feed: Normalise behavior, Bandwagon Effect
	-> Social proof/validation
- Friend Request & Tagging & Parties: 
	- Requests & Tagging: Social debt -> Reciprocity
	- Group behavior
	-> Subjective norm, Social Facilitation
- Simple cocktail post: leads to larger commitment, foot-in-the-door
- Profile Stats & Badges: Social comparison & Recognition

-- PERSUASIVE DESIGN & BEHAVIORAL NUDGES --
Make desired action easy and provide helpful transparent guidance
- Posting Simple:
	- Ability (FBM)
	- Simple flow -> Reduction
	- Suggestions -> Minimise cognitive load
- What can i make: Tailoring (PSD) and Facilitation (FBM)
	-> simplify decision for motivated but lack ability
- Notification defaults: 
	- Most ON by default
	- Maximise engagement
	- Transparent, automatic mind nudge
- Post visibility default:
	- Private as default selected
	- User autonomy
	- Ethical
- 1-5 star rating: External anchor, guide users perception and expectation

-- DARK PATTERNS --
Prioritise User Autonomy and Transparency
- Full control notifications
	-> no Roach Motel
- Default post privacy: Posts default to private
	-> no Privacy Zuckering
- No hidden costs: app is free to use
	-> no Deceptive Pricing
- Positively/Neutraly framed language (e.g. Dont use "No I hate having fun")
	-> no Confirmshaming

-- Product Led Growth (PLG) --
- Steak system: retention
- Share buttons: 
	- referral to external apps
	- Referral loop is tracked -> effectiveness of new Acquisition
- North star: 
	- Focus Activation & Usage
	- TTFA: First post
	- Northstar metric: % signed up users use at least 3 features within one week
